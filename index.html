My Baby Cheeto!

Cheeto is my cat, and he is now 1 year 8 months. His breed is domestic medium hair, and colors are black and white. He is a lovely, smart cat with nice face and big eyes. Also, he has good habits and nice character. He was born in California and was moved to a shelter in Seattle because of the big fire in CA,2018, in which, my boyfriend and I saw him in the first time.

First meeting:

It was on October 20,2017, we met him at the shelter in the first time. At first, we were interested in other cats while waiting. However, many cats were adopted by people who were earlier than us. When I was so disappointed and wanted to leave, Cheeto appeared. He was put into an empty glass case and looked at us. We immediately and certainly decided to adopt him at our first glance. That was Cheeto’s first day in Seattle, and the first day in a new family.

Days in Seattle:

Days in Seattle were normal, peaceful and with full nice memory. He got along well with us, and all guests to our apartment liked him. He slept everywhere at home, and sometimes he is so energetic to play his toys with us. In summer, he likes to stand or sleep at the case of laundry. Toilet always contracted him much. He walked along in kitchen and slept at my boyfriend’s chair at night. Our small apartment was filled with his
footprints, and I gradually got a feeling of family, my own little family at the U.S.

Moving & During trip:

We left Seattle at Jul.18, 2018(which is also my birthday) and drove to Athens for 6 days. That was not Cheeto’s first long trip, but this time he traveled by car. He acted much better than we imagined. Getting over the first few hours in car, he gradually adapted to our travel rhythm. He was not threatened by unknown things, but well-behaved and curious. He loved to see the views and scenes during the trip, and when we got bored or sleepy, he always replied to our calling, which added much pleasure to our journey.

Time at Athens:

Now, he is so happy everyday by running back and forth at home, seeing views standing by windows, and watching human activities that my roommates and I do. We live in a bigger apartment and he got new big cat tree, new toys, new treats (I allow him to have some everyday now), and many new friends come to my apartment to see him. He is shy to meet new people, but he really loves to stay with people.

His accompany is one of my best treasures at the U.S (another is my boyfriend). I am so appreciated that Cheeto and his daddy appeared into my life. Love both of you.
